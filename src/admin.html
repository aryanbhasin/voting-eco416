Steps to configure voting:

  • Step 1: Unlock your account by pasting in your account address below and clicking 'Unlock'
  • Step 2: Register voters one-by-one below by pasting in their account address and clicking 'Register Voter'
  • Step 2.1: To check if a voter is registered, paste in their account address under "Check registration of Voter"
  • Step 3: Register the different proposals you want to vote between by typing in a description and clicking 'Register'
  • Step 4: Once you've registered all voters & proposals, click "Start voting" & refresh the page to confirm the new status
  • Step 5: Come back after your voters have voted on the proposal and press "End voting" to restrict further voting
